For Trainers

Bring Your Clients. Build Your Business.

Already coaching your own clients? Train them in a space that actually reflects the quality of your work — clean, welcoming, low-crowd, and open 24/7, right here in New Brighton.

The Opportunity

A Home Base for Independent Trainers

DM Total Fitness was built by two trainers, for people who take their training seriously. If you run your own coaching business and want a better environment to bring your clients into, we'd love to talk.

You keep your clients and your coaching style — we provide the room to do your best work: quality equipment, a professional atmosphere, and a community that makes people want to come back. No giant chain energy, no fighting for machines, no corporate red tape. Just a great place to train.

The Environment

A Space Your Clients Will Love

The room you train in says a lot about you. Ours is built to make your clients feel comfortable, focused, and looked after.

24/7 Access

Train clients on your schedule — early mornings, evenings, weekends. The gym works around your business, not the other way around.

Quality Equipment

Free weights, machines, and functional training gear in a clean, well-kept space — everything you need to run a real session.

Low-Crowd, Not Low-Energy

A smaller, focused gym means no waiting on machines and no overwhelming crowds — your clients get room to move and your full attention.

Our Mission & Values

We Care About the Same Things You Do

DM Total Fitness exists to help our New Brighton neighbors get stronger, healthier, and more confident — with real coaching and zero intimidation. If that sounds like how you train, you'll fit right in.

No Judgment

Every body and every starting point belongs here. Your clients will feel welcome from the moment they walk in.

Results-Driven

We're here to help people actually reach their goals — the same outcome you're working toward with every client.

Real Community

Owner-operated and personal — you're working alongside people who know your name, not a faceless corporate gym.
