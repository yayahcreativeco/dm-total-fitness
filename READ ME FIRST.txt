============================================================
   DM TOTAL FITNESS  —  WEBSITE PREVIEW
   How to view the website on your computer
============================================================

This is a preview of your new website. It runs right from your
computer — it is NOT live on the internet yet. You can click
through every page exactly like a real website.

------------------------------------------------------------
TO OPEN IT (takes 10 seconds):
------------------------------------------------------------

1. UNZIP this folder if you haven't already.
   - On Windows: right-click the .zip file > "Extract All"
   - On Mac: double-click the .zip file

2. Open the unzipped "DM-Total-Fitness-Website" folder.

3. Find the file named:  index.html
   (This is the HOME page. It may just show as "index".)

4. DOUBLE-CLICK index.html.
   It will open in your web browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge, etc.).

That's it — you're now viewing the website!

------------------------------------------------------------
HOW TO LOOK AROUND:
------------------------------------------------------------

- Use the MENU at the top (Home, About, Services, Blog,
  Contact) to move between pages — just like a real site.

- To see the MEMBER PORTAL demo:
    1. Click "Member Login" in the top menu.
    2. Click the "Log In" button (any email/password works —
       it's a demo).
    3. You'll see a sample client dashboard with weight,
       measurements, strength progress, photos, and billing.
       (The numbers are placeholders to show the idea.)

- Try it on your PHONE too: the site adjusts to fit any screen.

------------------------------------------------------------
GOOD TO KNOW:
------------------------------------------------------------

- Best viewed in Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Safari, or
  Firefox. (Keep your internet on so the map and fonts load.)

- The contact form is a demo — it pops up a confirmation but
  doesn't send email yet. On the live site it will email you.

- A few spots are intentionally left blank for you to fill in:
  your bios, your photos, and your pricing. Just send those
  over to Sama whenever you're ready.

Questions? Reply to Sama's email anytime.
