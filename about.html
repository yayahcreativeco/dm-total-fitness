About Us

Real People. Real Coaching.

We're a smaller gym with a bigger purpose — helping our New Brighton neighbors get stronger, healthier, and more confident, one session at a time.

Our Story

A Gym That Feels Personal

DM Total Fitness was founded in 2026 by Damon and Markus, two trainers who believe getting in shape shouldn't mean fighting for equipment in a crowded warehouse or locking into a contract you can't escape.

So they built the gym they always wanted to train in: a clean, well-equipped space in New Brighton where members get genuine coaching, flexible 24/7 access, and a welcoming community — without the intimidation of a big-box chain.

Whether you're chasing a major transformation or just want a no-pressure place to stay healthy, you'll be known by name here and supported every step of the way.
